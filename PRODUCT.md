# PRODUCT.md - RevOps Dashboard

## Product Purpose
A revenue operations dashboard for tracking weekly KPIs including pipeline health, conversion rates, customer retention, and delivery metrics. Used by revenue operations teams, finance, and product leaders to monitor business health and spot trends.

## Users
- Revenue operations managers
- Finance analysts  
- Product leads
- Executive stakeholders

## Tone
Professional, data-first, clear. No fluff - metrics speak for themselves.

## Brand
- **Register**: product (app UI)
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS dashboards with gradient hero metrics, excessive cards, "growth hacking" aesthetics, animated confetti on milestones
- **Strategic Principles**:
  1. Data density without clutter - show the signal, hide the noise
  2. Comparison context - every metric needs a baseline comparison
  3. Trust through transparency - show data sources, forecast confidence
  4. Actionable, not decorative - every visual element serves a decision

## Visual Direction
- Clean, controlled palette with single accent color
- Charts should be readable at a glance
- Typography hierarchy through size and weight, not color
- Dark mode for extended use (observability context)
